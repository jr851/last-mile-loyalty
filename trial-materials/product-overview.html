The honest problem

Most café loyalty apps do the basics. No more.

Most digital stamp card tools for independent cafés count stamps and reset. The tools that drive real repeat behaviour — targeted promotions, broadcast messaging, offer logic — have traditionally only been available to chains with enterprise budgets.

What most loyalty apps give you

A stamp counter. A reset button. Maybe a dashboard. What's available to most independents today is a digital version of the paper punch card — without the offer logic, messaging tools, or promotion mechanics that make loyalty actually work.

What independent cafés actually need

The ability to drive footfall on a quiet Tuesday. To tell your regulars about Friday's special. To run a double-stamps event without printing new cards. The tools that make loyalty work — not just track it.

Getting started

Up and running in under ten minutes.

No technical setup. No hardware. No waiting.

1

Sign up in 2 minutes

Choose your café name, brand colour, stamp target, and reward. Done.

2

Print your counter sign

Download your unique QR code and print the A5 counter card template. Place on counter.

3

Customers scan and join

One scan, name and mobile number — they're enrolled with their first stamp in under 30 seconds. They then save their card to their home screen for instant access on every future visit.

4

Staff scan to stamp

Customer opens their home screen card and shows their QR. Staff scan with any phone camera, enter PIN, tap "Add stamp". About ten seconds per visit.

📌 How customers find their card on every visit

After joining, customers save their card to their phone's home screen. One tap opens their card instantly — no app, no login, no searching.

Android

Lost their card?

Re-scan the counter QR with the same mobile number — stamps restored instantly.
